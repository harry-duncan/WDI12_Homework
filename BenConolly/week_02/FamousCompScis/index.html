Alan Turing is an english mathematician who took an interesting in the possibility of computational machines to solve problems, building them from a mathematical base. He is most famous for, and more often portrated in popular culture, for his work on cracking the enigma code.

Unfortunately, Alan's contributions to computer science were cut short when he took his own life, after persecution he experienced for being a homosexual.
